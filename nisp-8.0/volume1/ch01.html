Chapter 1. Introduction

1. The NATO Interoperability Standards and Profiles (NISP), is developed by the NATO Consultation, Command and Control (C3) Board Interoperability Profiles Capability Team (IP CaT) and the current version, ADatP-34(G), was approved by the C3 Board[1]. The included interoperability standards (Volume 2) and profiles (Volume 3) will be mandatory for use in NATO common funded Communications and Information Systems (CIS). The NISP will be made available to the general public as ADatP-34(H) when approved by the C3 Board.


[1] AC/322-N(2013)0026-REV1-AS1
