3.2. Comparison to former NISP Versions

14. In comparison to the former version, this NISP is structured following the C3 Classification Taxonomy, as published by the C3B in June 2012. To allow a transformation from the old to this new structure, automatic tools were used. Nevertheless, not all entries (neither in the old, nor in the new structure) are well placed, as they are artificially assigned to the structure. A pure service oriented approach will lead to the result that these old entries will disapear in the future, when the relevant systems, where these standards were used, become obsolete.
