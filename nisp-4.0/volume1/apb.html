 B.1. Introduction

72. Updating of the NISP and its associated database will be conducted by a managed, rolling review process which will take into account information on standards available from a wide variety of sources. The NOSWG acts as the hub for this maintenance activity, supported by the NHQC3Staff and NC3A personnel as required.
