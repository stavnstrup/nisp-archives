 4.4. National Systems Interoperability Coordination

60. Each of the national NOSWG representatives is responsible for:

  • Providing the NOSWG with the appropriate and timely inputs with respect to interoperability with national systems;

  • Co-ordination of the national position, including co-ordination with national representatives of other sub-committees;

  • Providing the NOSWG with the appropriate technical information based on the national IT market assessment.
