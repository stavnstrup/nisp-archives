163. This purpose of this chapter is to specify the NISP mid term emerging standards. The document organises these standards into five service areas. These service areas are listed in volume 2.

164. This section presents all associated standards in tabular form. The tables refine each service area into one or more service categories, with service components mapping to the emerging mid term category (see NISP vol. 1). A remarks column provides optional supplementary information on each standard plus CCEB-specific information. The NISP Rationale Document (RD) provides further explanation on why service and standards categories have been selected.
