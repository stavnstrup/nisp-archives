3.3. User Information Services

15. These services are hierarchically organised and focused on information: content, management, processes, and standards. Each Information Service is enabled by one or more NNEC Services.

16. These NNEC services represent the technology required to enable the Information Services to make information available to user communities of interest. At the lowest level, NNEC Services are composed of components, processes, management.
